ABOUT

It was one particularly warm night on March 2, 2008, at the Cajundome in Lafayette, Louisiana. Somewhere in the nosebleeds stood an adolescent Candace and AJ, nerves buzzing with a dangerous mixture of excitement, nausea, and something else unnamed, balls of their feet bouncing up and down on the vibrating concrete. When the Jonas Brothers rose from beneath the kinetic stage, all curls and guitars and purity rings, and the involuntary screams from 12,000 other teenaged girls pierced through the ever-present humidity, the pair knew that no other earthly experience would ever be able to top this feeling, this moment, and we would spend the rest of our lives recreating it.
